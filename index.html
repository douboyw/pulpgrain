Our Story

At Pulp & Grain, we believe in the timeless beauty of natural materials. Each piece we create tells a story, combining the delicate intricacy of paper art with the organic warmth of hand-selected wood.

Our artisans bring years of experience and boundless creativity to every project, whether it's a custom wall installation, a one-of-a-kind gift, or a collaborative piece for your space.

We source sustainable materials and use traditional techniques merged with contemporary design to create art that connects people with nature.
